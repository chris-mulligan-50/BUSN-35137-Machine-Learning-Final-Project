Now finance вҖ“ two main challenges to factor research:

в–¶ Results replicate in-sample, but are spurious due to вҖңp-hackingвҖқ
вҖңMost claimed research findings in financial economics are likely falseвҖқ
вҖ“ Harvey et al. (2016)вҶ’replication rate:<50%
